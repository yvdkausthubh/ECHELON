About Us

ECHELON is a dynamic technical club that serves as the heart of innovation and collaboration for the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), while also embracing participation from other departments. Our club is a vibrant hub where students unite to transform ideas into reality, working on cutting-edge projects and fostering technical excellence.

At ECHELON, our primary focus is on executing impactful projects in the realms of electronics and software development. By integrating theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience, we empower students to become problem solvers and innovators, ready to tackle real-world challenges.

Vision

The Electronics and Communication Engineering Department intends to be a leader in creating high-quality engineers in the field of electronics and associated technologies to cater to national and global technological needs, promoting human prosperity and well-being.

Motto

To create exhibitions where students can showcase their ideas, build best-in-class projects, and organize workshops to promote new-age tools and technologies. Encouraging cross-department collaborations to drive innovation.
